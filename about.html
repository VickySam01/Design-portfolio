About Me

I'm Victoria Samuel, a creative product designer for about 2 years with a passion for crafting interactive and engaging digital experiences and facilitating design classes.

I haven't always been in the design space, despite having a tech degree. I started out in academics as i've always been one that likes to pass knowledge across one way or another. I thought about design when i noticed i could easily spot when objects were not properly aligned whether on digital of physical spaces. This has helped me to incorporate this in my designs where I do my best to pay attention to details.

I believe in people being at the core of life and I've been able to translate this to empathy when it comes to my designs where i put users front and center. It also and can also help me create designs for any/ most domains as users are at the core. This helps fuel my passion and keeps my energy up to see projects go from ideation to implementation and adoption.

When i'm not creating user-centric, impactful and visually appealing designs, I feed my eyes with beautiful interior home decor, read random book titles, explore my culinary prowess and have big dreams of when I'll tour the world.
